# Glossary – Cleaner App Australia

This glossary defines the main terms used throughout the project.  
It helps maintain clarity and consistency in documentation and development.

---

### **Job**
A cleaning task assigned to a cleaner. Includes client details, address, date, time, checklist, photos, and time tracking.

### **Checklist**
A predefined list of cleaning tasks for different areas (bathroom, kitchen, living room, etc.) that cleaners must complete during a job.

### **Task**
An individual item within a checklist (e.g., “Clean sink”, “Vacuum floor”).

### **Cleaner**
A worker who performs cleaning jobs. Primary user of the app.

### **Supervisor / Team Leader**
A small-business owner or team manager who reviews the work of cleaners, checks photos, verifies quality, and manages assignments.

### **Client**
A homeowner, tenant, landlord, Airbnb host, or office manager who requests cleaning services. They receive reports and invoices but may not use the app directly.

### **Photos (Proof of Work)**
Pictures taken by the cleaner during or after the job to demonstrate that the cleaning tasks were completed properly.

### **Time Tracking**
A feature that measures the duration of a job using start/stop timers.

### **Report**
A summary of the cleaning job, including tasks completed, photos, time taken, and any notes.

### **MVP (Minimum Viable Product)**
The simplest version of the app that delivers real value: job creation, checklists, photos, time tracking, and simple invoices.

### **Dashboard**
A view for supervisors with information such as job history, cleaner activity, and performance.


---

If additional terms arise during development, they should be added to this document to keep the project consistent.
